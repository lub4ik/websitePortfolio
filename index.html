Why study Astronomy?
26 October, 2019

People have been looking at the starry skies for thousands years. We always ask questions, whether we are alone in tremendous universe or not, how the life started and how it will end?

Astronomy is a science that seeks to explain everything that we observe in the Universe. By studying the cosmos beyond our planet, we can find an answer to the question, how different laws of physics work under conditions which are impossible on our planet.

Beautiful sprinkling of stars in the clear sky awakens our fundamental curiosity about the purpose of our life and its origin.
